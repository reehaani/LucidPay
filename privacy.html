Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Data We Handle

The extension accesses StreamElements pages to automate alert imports and may read session tokens (JWT) needed for Quick Scan Connect. These values are used to generate a QR code and are stored locally in your browser.

Storage

  • Local storage may include import progress, logs, and QR credentials.
  • No data is transmitted to third-party servers by the extension.

Third-Party Services

StreamElements is a third-party service. This extension is not affiliated with StreamElements.
